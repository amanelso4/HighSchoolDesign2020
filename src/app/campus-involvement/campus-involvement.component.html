Women In Computing

Pictured above is myself volunteering at a GirlCode event. At these events, we invite middle school girls to the School of Engineering and teach them how to make simple programs in Python using Raspberry Pi's. Our mission is to empower young girls to pursue a career in tech. The number of participants continues to grow with each GirlCode event thanks to all the passion and time the Women and Computing college students put into this program.
